# PantryPilot Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-06-06

PantryPilot is an offline-first pantry, grocery-list, package-label review, and
meal-idea app.

## Data PantryPilot Handles

PantryPilot lets users enter and store:

- pantry item names, quantities, categories, storage locations, and expiry dates;
- grocery-list items and checked status;
- text pasted into the package-label parser;
- locally selected or captured package images; and
- app settings, tutorial state, and local demo entitlement state.

PantryPilot does not automatically collect, upload, sell, or share this data.
The current app does not use accounts, internet access, analytics SDKs, live
advertising SDKs, device-location permissions, contacts, notifications, or
Google Play Billing.

## Local Processing And Storage

Pantry and grocery data, settings, and demo entitlement state are stored in
PantryPilot's private app storage on the device.

Selected package images and pasted label text are processed locally. Imported
images are not copied into permanent PantryPilot storage. Photos captured
through a camera app may be temporarily stored in PantryPilot's private cache
and may be removed automatically by Android.

PantryPilot does not transmit locally processed content to a PantryPilot server.

## Camera, Photo Picker, And Sharing

PantryPilot can open a camera app or Android document picker when the user
requests those features. Those external apps are governed by their own privacy
practices.

PantryPilot can open Android's share sheet when the user chooses to share a
pantry or grocery list. The selected receiving app then receives the text the
user chose to share. PantryPilot does not otherwise share this data.

## Purchases And Advertising

The current build includes local demo purchase controls for testing plan gates
and the Remove Ads entitlement. They do not charge money, contact Google Play,
or collect payment information.

The Free plan includes a sponsor-banner placeholder. The current build does not
include a live advertising SDK and does not load advertisements.

Before a future release enables Google Play Billing, AdMob, analytics, online
services, or other third-party SDKs, this policy and the Google Play Data safety
declarations will be updated to describe their data handling.

## Security

PantryPilot stores its app data in Android private app storage and disables
Android backup. The current build does not transmit PantryPilot data over a
network.

## Retention And Deletion

Local app data remains on the device until the user changes or removes it,
clears PantryPilot's app storage, or uninstalls PantryPilot. Temporary cached
images may be removed automatically by Android. PantryPilot has no accounts or
server-side user data to delete.

## Children's Privacy

PantryPilot is not designed specifically for children and does not knowingly
collect personal information from children.

## Changes To This Policy

This policy may be updated when PantryPilot's features or data practices
change. The effective date above identifies the latest revision.

## Contact

For privacy questions, use the developer support email shown on PantryPilot's
Google Play store listing.
