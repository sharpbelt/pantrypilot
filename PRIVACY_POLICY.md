# PantryPilot Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-06-06

PantryPilot is an offline-first pantry, grocery-list, package-label review, and
meal-idea app.

## Data PantryPilot Handles

PantryPilot lets users enter and store:

- pantry item names, quantities, categories, storage locations, and expiry dates;
- grocery-list items and checked status;
- text pasted into the package-label parser;
- locally selected or captured package images; and
- app settings, tutorial state, and local entitlement state.

PantryPilot does not upload pantry contents, grocery lists, package images, or
label text to a PantryPilot server. The app does not use accounts, device
location permissions, contacts, or notifications.

## Local Processing And Storage

Pantry and grocery data, settings, and entitlement state are stored in
PantryPilot's private app storage on the device.

Selected package images and pasted label text are processed locally. Imported
images are not copied into permanent PantryPilot storage. Photos captured
through a camera app may be temporarily stored in PantryPilot's private cache
and may be removed automatically by Android.

PantryPilot does not transmit locally processed pantry content to a PantryPilot
server. The advertising service described below processes separate device and
usage data.

## Camera, Photo Picker, And Sharing

PantryPilot can open a camera app or Android document picker when the user
requests those features. Those external apps are governed by their own privacy
practices.

PantryPilot can open Android's share sheet when the user chooses to share a
pantry or grocery list. The selected receiving app then receives the text the
user chose to share. PantryPilot does not otherwise share this data.

## Purchases

PantryPilot uses Google Play Billing for optional one-time digital upgrades:
Remove Ads, Plus, and Pro. Google Play handles checkout and payment information;
PantryPilot does not receive credit-card or bank-account details.

PantryPilot receives purchase status, product identifiers, and purchase tokens
from Google Play to grant, acknowledge, and restore owned upgrades. These are
processed locally by the app and Google Play. PantryPilot does not send them to
a PantryPilot server.

More information:

- Google Play Privacy Notice: https://policies.google.com/privacy
- Google Play purchase help: https://support.google.com/googleplay/answer/2850369

## Advertising And Consent

The Free plan uses Google AdMob to display banner advertisements. Remove Ads,
Plus, and Pro users do not receive these banner requests.

Google's Mobile Ads SDK may automatically collect and share:

- IP address, which may be used to estimate general location;
- app launches, taps, ad interactions, and video views;
- diagnostic information such as app launch time, hangs, and energy use; and
- device or account identifiers, including the Android advertising ID and app
  set ID when available.

Google uses this information for advertising, analytics, and fraud prevention.
Data transmitted by the Google Mobile Ads SDK is encrypted in transit.

PantryPilot uses Google's User Messaging Platform to request applicable privacy
choices before requesting ads. UK and EEA users can change applicable choices
through the **Ad privacy** control shown with the Free-plan advertising area.

More information:

- Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
- How Google uses information from sites or apps that use its services:
  https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

Before a future release enables analytics, online services, or other third-party
SDKs, this policy and the Google Play Data safety
declarations will be updated to describe their data handling.

## Security

PantryPilot stores its app data in Android private app storage and disables
Android backup. Locally processed pantry content is not sent to a PantryPilot
server. AdMob data is transmitted securely as described above.

## Retention And Deletion

Local app data remains on the device until the user changes or removes it,
clears PantryPilot's app storage, or uninstalls PantryPilot. Temporary cached
images may be removed automatically by Android. PantryPilot has no accounts or
server-side user data to delete.

## Children's Privacy

PantryPilot is not designed specifically for children and does not knowingly
collect personal information from children.

This policy may be updated when PantryPilot's features or data practices
change. The effective date above identifies the latest revision.
