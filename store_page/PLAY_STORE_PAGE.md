# PantryPilot Play Store Page

## Store Listing Fields

App name:

PantryPilot

Short description:

Track pantry food, expiry dates, groceries, and local meal ideas.

Full description:

PantryPilot keeps your pantry, fridge, freezer, grocery list, and meal planning
in one practical Android app.

Add food manually, review package photos, paste label text copied from camera
OCR or receipts, track expiry dates, and build grocery lists from what you are
missing. Pantry and grocery content stays in local app storage.

Core features:

- Pantry, fridge, freezer, counter, and other storage zones
- Expiry dates and expiring-soon summaries
- Categorized pantry cards with simple food icons
- Grocery lists grouped by category
- Add pantry items directly to the grocery list
- Photo-assisted item entry
- Local label text parser for package text, receipts, or notes
- Local meal ideas from pantry categories
- Share pantry inventory or grocery lists through Android's share sheet
- No account required

The Free plan includes starter limits and a small consent-gated banner ad.
Optional one-time Google Play upgrades can remove ads and unlock larger local
limits and additional tools:

- Remove Ads: keep Free limits without banner ads
- Plus: larger limits, photo review, label parsing, sharing, full meal ideas,
  and no ads
- Pro: highest limits, meal extras, and no ads

PantryPilot is a local-first kitchen organizer. It is not a diet tracker,
calorie tracker, cloud-sync service, or automatic package-image OCR service.
