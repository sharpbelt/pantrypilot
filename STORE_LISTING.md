Your smart pantry pal for scanning items, avoiding expired food, and planning meals from what you already have.

## Full Description

PantryPilot is your smart pantry assistant for keeping track of pantry, fridge, and freezer items, avoiding expired food, building grocery lists, and deciding what to cook next.

Instead of another aggressive diet or calorie app, PantryPilot focuses on practical home food organization: what you own, what expires soon, what you need to buy, and what meals make sense from the ingredients already in your kitchen.

Features:

- App-like dashboard with expandable pantry, expiry, grocery, and meal sections
- Photo-assisted item scanner flow: import photo or take photo, paste label text copied from camera OCR when available, review expiry date and quantity, then add pantry item
- Local label text parser for package text, receipts, or notes
- Pantry, fridge, freezer, and counter inventory
- Expiry date tracking
- Expiring-soon dashboard
- Categorized grocery list
- Add pantry items directly to grocery list
- Share pantry inventory and grocery lists as local text
- Local meal ideas from owned ingredients
- Offline-first local storage
- Free, Remove Ads, Plus, and Pro gates for testing upgrade packaging
- No accounts; Free users may receive consent-gated Google AdMob banner ads

Current plan draft:

- Free: core pantry/grocery tracking with starter limits and a small banner sponsor space
- Remove Ads: one-time ad-free unlock for Free users
- Plus: larger local limits, photo review, label text parsing, sharing, full meal idea list, and no ads
- Pro: highest local limits, meal-planning extras, and no ads

Play product ID draft:

- Remove Ads: `pantrypilot_remove_ads_one_time`
- Plus: `pantrypilot_plus_one_time`
- Pro: `pantrypilot_pro_one_time`

Do not publish public store copy that claims real paid purchases until the demo purchase flow is replaced with Google Play Billing. AdMob is integrated for Free users; keep the privacy policy and Play Data Safety answers aligned with the current Mobile Ads SDK.
