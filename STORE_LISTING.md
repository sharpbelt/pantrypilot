# PantryPilot Store Listing

The paste-ready public listing is maintained in:

`store_page/PLAY_STORE_PAGE.md`

Use its app name, short description, full description, category, contact
details, privacy URL, and screenshot selection.

Key publication boundaries:

- Upload screenshots 01 through 06 only.
- Do not claim automatic package-image OCR or AI recognition.
- Do not claim cloud sync, accounts, subscriptions, location reminders, or
  notifications.
- Google Play Billing and AdMob are integrated, but public production promotion
  remains gated on active products and real license-tester purchase results.
